About Us



Welcome to Beaver AUV! we are Beaver Country Day School's dedicated Robosub team. Our team spends all year working to build an autonomous underwater vehicle to compete in the National Robosub Competition. Beaver AUV was founded in 2015 and and since then each new generation of students has added, modified and improved upon the vehicle. This team has been to 4 competitions competing in several events that range from torpedo shooting to navigating gates.
